Cupcake as breakfast with chantilly

The perfect little cake for breakfast for everyday. Its so easy and wonderful, for sure its gone impress. Make and after tell me what you think about it.

Time: 1h10
Yield: 12 portions
Dificulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon of instant coffee
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 2/3 cup of cocoa powder
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 2 cups of ready-made whipped cream
  • Coffee beans for decoration
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of instant coffee
  • 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Preparation Mode

In a blender, blend the water, instant coffee, eggs, oil, sugar, and cocoa powder until smooth. Transfer to a bowl, add the flour and baking powder, and mix with a spoon. Pour the batter into cupcake liners placed inside a large baking tray, ensuring they are side by side. Bake in a preheated medium oven for 25 minutes or until baked and golden. Remove and let cool.

For the filling, combine all the ingredients in a medium saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened. Turn off the heat, let cool, and transfer to a piping bag fitted with a round tip. Set aside. Place the whipped cream into another piping bag with a star tip and set aside.

To assemble, make a hole in the center of each cupcake and fill with the reserved coffee-flavored brigadeiro. Decorate the tops of the cupcakes with the reserved whipped cream in circular patterns. Garnish with coffee beans and refrigerate until ready to serve.
